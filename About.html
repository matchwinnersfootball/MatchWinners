About Marcus Wright

Marcus Wright is a highly experienced elite football coach with a proven track record at imrpoving players holistic growth. with experience at Tottenham Hotspur and ten years of learning from arsenal methodology across various departments.at Arsenal FC. Known for developing match winners, Marcus combines modern coaching , social science with deep game understanding.

Mission

To empower young players with the tools, mindset, and confidence to thrive in real-game situations — whether they aim for grassroots success or academy level breakthroughs.

Approach

Marcus’s coaching blends technical development, decision-making, and game resilience. His sessions focus on decisive moments and game-winning actions.

Trusted by Families

Hundreds of players and parents trust Marcus to bring the best out of young footballers — both on and off the pitch.

Charity & Impact

Off the pitch, Marcus is proud to be an ambassador and mentor for Teens Unite having been diagnosed with stage 4 cancer at the age of 15 followed by 18 months of chemotherapy and radiotherapy marcus was determined to give back and help people that have gone through similar and worse. teens unite — a charity supporting young people living with and beyond cancer. Through a series of high-impact events, Marcus has helped raise over £500,000 to empower teens through sport, purpose, and community.
