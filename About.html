About Marcus Wright

Marcus Wright is a highly experienced elite football coach with a proven track record in improving players’ holistic growth. With experience at Tottenham Hotspur and ten years of learning from the methodology at Arsenal FC, Marcus is known for developing match winners through modern coaching, social science, and deep tactical understanding.

Mission

To empower young players with the tools, mindset, and confidence to thrive in real-game situations — whether they aim for grassroots success or academy-level breakthroughs.

Approach

Marcus’s coaching blends technical development, decision-making, and game resilience. His sessions focus on decisive moments and game-winning actions.

Trusted by Families

Hundreds of players and parents trust Marcus to bring the best out of young footballers — both on and off the pitch.

Charity & Impact

Off the pitch, Marcus is proud to be an ambassador and mentor for Teens Unite. Having been diagnosed with stage 4 cancer at 15 and undergoing 18 months of treatment, Marcus is determined to give back. Through a series of high-impact events, he has helped raise over £500,000 to empower teens through sport, purpose, and community.
